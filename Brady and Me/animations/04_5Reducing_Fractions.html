Example

Reduce 12/18 to its lowest terms. Two methods

Method 1: Find the greatest common factor and divide

Try 6. 12 divided by 6 is 2. 18 divided bty 6 is 3. The answer is 2/3

Method 2: Split the numerator and denoinator into is prime factorization and cancel

12 = 2 * 2 * 3 and 18 = 2 * 3 * 3. A 2 and a 3 cancel from the top and bottom. We get 2 /3
